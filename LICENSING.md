# Licensing Policy

## License

This toolbox is released under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0** (see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE)).

## What This Means in Practice

- **Academic research, teaching, and individual use.** Use, modify, and publish with the toolbox freely. We ask that you cite it (see [`CITATION.cff`](CITATION.cff)).
- **Industry-internal research and R&D.** Permitted under AGPL. AGPL's obligations attach to distribution and network conveyance to third parties; purely internal use of the toolbox (or modifications to it) does not require you to release source.
- **Publishing research that uses the toolbox.** Permitted, including conference and journal papers from corporate authors. Outputs of the toolbox (computed sensitivities, plots, fitted parameters, derived analyses) are not derivative works of the toolbox itself, so papers describing results obtained with the toolbox are outside AGPL's scope. We ask only that they cite it (see [`CITATION.cff`](CITATION.cff)). Note that distribution of *modified toolbox source*, for example as part of a paper's reproducibility artifacts, does fall under AGPL and would require those modifications to be released under AGPL.
- **Productization** (shipping a closed-source product, library, or SaaS that incorporates this toolbox) is not permitted under AGPL.

A separate commercial license is available on request for parties who want to incorporate the toolbox into closed-source products or services.

## Intent

The AGPL choice is intentional: it preserves the toolbox as a public research resource while reserving commercial productization for licensed partners.

For commercial licensing, or for questions about whether a particular use case falls inside AGPL's terms, please contact the maintainers. Maintainers and their institutional affiliations are listed in [`CITATION.cff`](CITATION.cff).
